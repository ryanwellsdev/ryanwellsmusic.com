Ryan Wells is a multifaceted veteran in Western Canada's house and techno underground, creating with purpose and unwavering dedication to his craft. His original tracks bring a contemporary hardware-based edge to foundational genres, with warm dance floor driven instrumentation, crisp acid lines, and gripping breaks. Wells is thrilled to unveil the culmination of his growth with a unique live set on programmable hardware consisting of his original music. He has been mentored by prominent Canadian pioneers like Richie Hawtin and Mathew Jonson discovering a new identity from within the studio's solace, crafting a distinctive musical expression.
